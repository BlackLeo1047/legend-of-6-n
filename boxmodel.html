The Legend of 6+n

The Former Leader of the Axe Gang

"You may challenge the streets, but never challenge 6+n."

The man behind that legendary saying was none other than 6+n, the former leader of the Axe Gang.

Legend has it that 6+n was not born a fearsome warrior. In his younger days, he was merely an unknown foot soldier. One day, armed with nothing more than an ordinary axe, he faced dozens of enemies alone.

No one knows what truly happened that day. When the sun finally set, the entire street had fallen silent, with only the wind whispering through the empty alleyways. From that moment on, a legend was born.

Some say his axe struck faster than lightning. Others insist he never even needed to swing it. All he had to do was adjust his glasses, and his opponents would lose the will to fight.

Yet what truly earned him respect was not his strength— it was his code of honor.

  • Never bully the weak.
  • Never betray your brothers.
  • Always take a picture before eating.

Under his leadership, the Axe Gang became more than just a feared organization. It became a brotherhood built on loyalty and respect.

Years later, after one final battle that changed the underworld forever, 6+n chose to walk away. He claimed no riches. He left behind no grand farewell. He carried only a black coat, a pair of worn sneakers, and the old axe that had accompanied him throughout his life.

Nowadays, people occasionally claim to see a man dressed in black, quietly smiling in a small café or standing by a quiet street corner. He no longer commands an empire. He no longer fights for territory. He simply enjoys a peaceful cup of coffee and the ordinary life he once could never have.

"Boss... the underworld still remembers you."

He simply adjusts his glasses, smiles faintly, and replies:

"These days... I just want to enjoy a good meal."

The name 6+n lives on—not merely as a former gang leader, but as a legend.
